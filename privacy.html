Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

1. Overview

AI Solo OS is a public 90-day experiment about building a lightweight operating system for solo founders using AI.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you visit this website, subscribe to the newsletter, or interact with AI Solo OS content.

This is a minimal privacy notice for an early-stage project. It may be updated as the project grows or as new tools and services are added.

2. Information We May Collect

AI Solo OS does not currently require user accounts or direct login on this website.

However, the following types of information may be collected depending on how you interact with the project:

  • Email address, if you subscribe to the AI Solo OS newsletter.
  • Basic website usage data, such as page visits or referral sources.
  • Information you voluntarily provide if you reply to emails, contact the project, or interact through external platforms.

3. Newsletter and Email

AI Solo OS uses Beehiiv to manage newsletter subscriptions and email delivery.

If you subscribe to the newsletter, your email address and subscription-related data may be processed by Beehiiv according to its own privacy practices.

You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time by using the unsubscribe link included in newsletter emails.

4. Third-Party Services

AI Solo OS may use third-party services to operate the website, publish content, manage subscriptions, or share updates.

These may include:

  • Cloudflare Pages for website hosting and deployment.
  • GitHub for source code and project file management.
  • Beehiiv for newsletter subscriptions and email delivery.
  • X for Build-in-Public updates and public communication.
  • Gumroad for possible future product distribution.

These third-party services may collect data according to their own privacy policies and terms.

5. Cookies and Analytics

AI Solo OS may use basic analytics or hosting-related logs to understand website traffic and improve the project.

At this stage, the project does not use complex tracking systems or advertising pixels. If analytics, cookies, or tracking tools are added later, this policy will be updated.

6. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

  • Send newsletter updates to subscribers.
  • Improve the website and project content.
  • Understand what resources are useful to visitors.
  • Maintain basic security, hosting, and site performance.
  • Prepare future resources, templates, or products related to AI Solo OS.

7. Data Sharing

AI Solo OS does not sell personal information.

Information may be processed by third-party tools used to operate the project, such as newsletter, hosting, analytics, or payment platforms.

8. Data Retention

Newsletter subscription data may be retained as long as you remain subscribed.

You may unsubscribe at any time through the link provided in newsletter emails.

9. Your Choices

You can choose not to subscribe to the newsletter or not to interact with external platforms.

If you are subscribed to the newsletter, you can unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link in emails.

10. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as AI Solo OS evolves, especially if new tools, analytics, products, or payment systems are added.

The latest version will be posted on this page.

11. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or AI Solo OS, you can contact or follow the project through:

  • Website: https://aisoloos.com
  • Newsletter: https://aisoloos.beehiiv.com/
